Well... nobody's posted any new words so I guess I will.

* everywhere - **tokatokre**
* somewhere - **toatokre**
* no where - **bokatokre**

* always, forever - **tokakrone**
* sometime - **toakrone**
* never - **bokakrone**

* to jump - **shonag**
* to kill - **krozag**
* to get, recieve - **handag**
* to take - **tag**
* to walk - **promag**
* to run - **haiag**

* car - **aute**
* bike - **kiane**
