## [[qbri|QBri]] writes again:

New Words:

* **Mikare** - Hero
* **Koiag** - to open
* **Katsae** - door (from kamitsae)
* **Diskelag** - to descend
* **Sakse** - stone, rock (from latin "saxum")
* **Tasag** - stand
* **Ludag** - to play (an instrument)
* **Rege** - king
* **Kafulag** - to beat, defeat
* **Ablat** - prep. "by, by way of"

That's all I got for now.
