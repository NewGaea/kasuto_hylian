# akantroshe

# Neo-Modern Hylian
## Alternative Forms

## Etymology
From [*akana*](lexicon/lexeme/a/akana) "present, current" + [*troshe*](lexicon/lexemes/t/troshe) "tense, (point in) time".

## Pronunciation
- **IPA Broad**: /,a.kan.'tro.ʃe/

## Noun

### akantroshe (plural *akantroshën*)
1. present time
2. (grammar) present tense
